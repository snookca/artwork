Rockefeller Center

I am pretty sure I came across this photo on Reddit and when I found that it was available for download, I grabbed a high resolution graphic file and sent it to a printer.

New York City views. Rockefeller Center and RCA Building from 515 Madison Ave. Taken by Gottscho on December 3, 1933. Gottscho's photos are in the public domain and made available via the Library of Congress.

Artist: Samuel H. Gottscho
24" x 36"
